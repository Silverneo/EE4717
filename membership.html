Membership Benefits

Workout the way you want, and get the most from the latest fitness equipment, group workouts and dedicated support from our certified experts. Your membership includes everything you need to keep fit & be Fit-tastic of your life. Here are some highlights of your benefits.

ASSISTANCE FROM EXPERTS

If you do not know what you are doing when it comes to exercise, you may see limited results or even injure yourself. Joining us allows you access to fitness professionals, such as personal trainers, aerobics instructors and nutritional experts. Our experts can help you develop a correct exercise program.

GROUP EXERCISE CLASSES

Some classes offered in our gym include cycling classes, dance classes, yoga and water aerobics. All exercise classes are free, so try them all! You will get nothing to lose by trying something new. Trying a variety of classes can help prevent boredom and you may even find something you love.

ACCESS TO EQUIPMENT & FACILITIES

We provide a wide variety of equipment for you to choose from. For instance, various types of weight machines that work almost every body part can be found. Cardiovascular machines, such as treadmills, bikes and stair climbers are also usually standard equipment in most gyms.

AND LOTS OF EXTRA PERKS...

We all know that training with a friend means more motivation, more fun and no more excuses. Just bring your friend and come down to give a try! Your first training is FREE!
